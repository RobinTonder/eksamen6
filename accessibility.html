Welcome page

The report shows that we got 0 errors on the homepage. However, we can see 3 contrast errors. These say that we have very low contrast between "Welcome to Pilestredet Hall", "Book Table" , "See the menu" and the background color. We don't actually see the background color, because we are using a background image, so we have decided to ignore these errors since the contrast looks good and the texts are easily readable. We tried the suggested color from the report, but the text blends too much into the background image. The alert symbol is just telling us that the "Welcome" button is redirecting to the homepage.

Booking page

The report regarding our "Booking" page flagged 12 errors. All of them were for missing label for the form. Labels have been added and should fix the accessibility and make it more readable and eyecatching. 2 Alerts; one is missing heading and we have added that; "Information" on top of the page. Second alert is "Booking" buttons is redirecting to the same page

Menu/Cafe page

No errors on the menu/cafe page. We remembered to put "alt" text to every image since this was one of the comments we got during our past assignments. We also put in "title" text to every image to make it even more readable. Alert: "Cafe/Menu" button is redirecting to same page

Topical page

No errors on the topical page. The reports shows that we have three contrast errors. All of them are regarding links to external sites or PDF. A solution to making the constrast better was to make the link-text bold. After doing this, the links much more visible and more readable which increases the accessibility. The color of the links were brighter and looked more visible, but we changed it to a darker color to remove the contrast error alert. Alerts: 1. is redirecting to same page. 2. PDF link.

Group thoughts: From the very beginning, we had in mind that the website should be easily readable by everyone. Some actions we took was to make sure that background image in the "Welcome" page was dark enough for the "Welcome to..." text to be easily readable in a bright front. This makes it harder for the text to blend into the background image. By doing this; we achieved a very readably welcome text. Another idea we had was to make less important text more aesthetic. This is why the "Pilestredet Hall" from the banner and "Welcome text" uses different font to make it more appealing. The rest of the website uses regular font since that info is more important to read and understand. Someplaces uses also bold font to make it even more readable.
