Welcome to Pilestredet Hall.

We are a 5 star venue that is famous in Norway for its good food and nice nature.
Pilestredet Hall is a trendy place, with a wonderful traditional designed house that is 1,051 square feet.
The establishment seats up to 75 people and is located in the center of Oslo.
Here you can make reservations to the most incredible spaces for meetings, conferences, parties and weddings.
The view from every room is very beautiful and is something you should be excited for every morning.
The venue also includes a café which is open for everyone.
